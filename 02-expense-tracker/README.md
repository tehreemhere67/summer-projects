"Imagine you want to track your spending this month. Every time you spend money, you open this program and log it. 
So you'd say "I spent 500 rupees on food" and the program saves that. Then later you can open it and see everything you've spent, and it'll also tell you "you spent 2000 rupees total on food, 800 on transport" etc.
All of this gets saved to a CSV file. So your data doesn't disappear when you close the program, it's always there.
It's basically a simple budgeting app but in the terminal. "
